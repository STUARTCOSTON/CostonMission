All About Birth

We serve an awesome God.

He provides for us when we don't ask for it, he protects us without us knowing about it, and he blesses us even when we don't deserve it. Of his own will he chose us to be his children and caused us to be born again. This concept has become very familiar to us recently.

Looking back over the last few months it rang clear that the theme of our lives has been

almost exclusively about birth. The birth Jesus, the birth of the New Year, and the birth of our third child.

Each one of these births has had a different impact on our lives and provided us with different experiences and new perspectives. As we unveil these three events through this newsletter we hope to give you a look into our lives and a picture of what God is doing in and through us as a family all the time.
